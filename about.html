About Us

Our Story

Founded over 15 years ago in the heart of Dubai, Raja & Tahir Transport L.L.C started with a single truck and a mission to make deliveries safer, faster, and more reliable. From a small family-run business, we’ve grown into a trusted name throughout the UAE and Oman.

Where We Operate

We serve across the UAE and Oman, including key cities like Dubai, Abu Dhabi, Sharjah, Ajman, Muscat, and Sohar. Our knowledge of routes helps us deliver promptly, no matter the destination.

What We Deliver

We specialize in transporting foodstuff, heavy goods, and construction materials. Our modern fleet consists of box trucks and trailers ready to handle deliveries of any scale.

Our Core Values

  • Safety: Your cargo is handled with care from start to finish.
  • Punctuality: We respect deadlines and deliver as promised.
  • Customer Satisfaction: We value our customers as partners and strive to exceed expectations.

Looking Ahead

Over the next decade, we aim to grow our network, expand our fleet, and build lasting relationships with new customers. With dedication and a people-first approach, we’re driving towards a brighter future.
