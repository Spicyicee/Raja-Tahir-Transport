About Raja & Tahir Transport L.L.C

Founded over 15 years ago, Raja & Tahir Transport L.L.C has established itself as a trusted leader in logistics and transportation across the UAE and Oman. We specialize in delivering a wide range of goods including foodstuff, labor cabins, steel and accessories, tires, and security cabins. Our commitment to excellence and customer satisfaction has helped us grow a strong reputation in the industry.

What sets us apart is our unwavering focus on safety and punctuality. With a modern fleet of trucks and highly experienced drivers, we ensure your shipments reach their destinations securely and on time, every time.
